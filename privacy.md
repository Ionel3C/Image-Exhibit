# Privacy Policy for Image Exhibit

**Last updated:** December 10, 2025

Image Exhibit is a fully offline Android application that displays and auto-randomizes wallpapers.

## Data Collection
This app **does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data**.  
It **does not access the internet** during normal operation.

## Permissions
The app only requests storage permissions to:
- Set wallpapers on your device
- Access images in your "Custom" folder (which you choose)

All images remain **100% on your device**.

## Updates
App updates are delivered exclusively through the Google Play Store. No telemetry or usage data is sent.
